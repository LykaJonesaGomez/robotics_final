Workforce Career Institute

Profile

Workforce Career Institute is a non-stock, non-profit educational institution focused on preparing individuals for careers in various industries. It offers a variety of programs aimed at developing specific skills and knowledge relevant to today’s workplace requirements. It further guarantees holistic services and support to its students from admission to graduation.

  • Career Mapping Sessions
  • Dynamic and Collaborative Classroom Environment
  • Mentoring and Coaching Sessions
  • Career Support Programs

Workforce Career Institute is the Founder’s dream come true. It all started with an ardent desire to put up an education institution that would cater to the needs of Filipinos from career programs to graduate programs.

The pandemic changed the education landscape in the country.

The situation inspired the Founder, Dr. Peter Elmar Cahati-an Bordo, to put up an institution that serves as a platform for the continuous delivery of pandemic-proof programs.

Now, Workforce Career Institute is serving its purpose to the Filipino people through the delivery of various VocTech, Bachelor’s, Master’s, and Doctoral programs which give hope to those who are enthusiastic in learning and imbibing careerpreneurship spirit.

Vision

It envisions itself to be the premier career-focused institution in Aklan by helping individuals build and refine their skillsets and become job-ready.

Mission

  • Develop and implement industry-based programs enabling individuals to cope with the ever-changing needs of their respective industries.
  • Provide practical, real-world education bridging the gap between theory and application.
  • Build strong, dynamic partnerships with various industry players to support students’ job search efforts.

Core Values

  • Advocate of distance education and computer-aided learning
  • Believer in lifelong learning
  • Committed to providing quality teaching
  • Dedicated to uplifting teamwork spirit
  • Excellence in post-study support
